A data portrait built to be vandalized.

I moved to New York and couldn't stop looking at the walls. Every surface tagged, stickered, pasted over—layers of people saying I was here, this is me, I made this. The city calls it vandalism. I see the most honest portrait format that exists.

De//Faced is a 3D sculpture of my head, rotating, waiting—built to be marked up.

My identity only becomes visible when you choose to mark it. Select a prompt to generate a fragment of my practice—how I start, what I make when nobody’s watching, or what I can’t stop thinking about. Gemini will translate the choice into a sticker and slap it onto the surface.

You don’t observe it. You leave something on it.
